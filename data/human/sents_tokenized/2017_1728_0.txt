The most recent research studies into the long - term landscape evolution of the Abruzzo area , carried out over the last twenty years at the " G . d ' Annunzio " University of Chieti - Pescara , are based on an integrated approach incorporating structural geology and geomorphology and , in particular , the geomorphometry of topographic and hydrographic aspects , geological and structural - geomorphological surveys and mapping supported by morpho - stratigraphic and chronological constraints .
