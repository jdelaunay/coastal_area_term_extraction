In this study , Tetragonia tetragonoides seedlings were sand - cultured with nutritional solution of different salinities ( 0 , 100 , 200 , 300 , 400 , and 600 mmol L - ' ) , and the seedling growth indices , biomass , and photosynthetic and chlorophyll fluorescence parameters were determined after 7 - week growth , aimed to approach the salt tolerance of T . tetragonoides .
