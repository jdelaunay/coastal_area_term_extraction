The long - term cliff recession rates estimated from shore platform widths ( 1 . 4 0 . 1 to 14 . 3 0 . 1 mm y - 1 ) are consistent with the lower end of the average range of cliff top and face recession rates published for Waitemata Group rocks using different methods ( 11 - 75 mm y - 1 ) , and in agreement with cliff base recession estimates ( 3 . 5 mm y - 1 ) .
