Adults and deutonymphs of a new water mite species of the genus Hygrobates Koch , 1837 ( Acari , Hydrachnidia , Hygrobatidae ) were found to parasitize the Hong Kong Newt , Paramesotriton hongkongensis Myers and Leviton , 1962 ( Amphibia , Caudata , Salamandridae ) , endemic to Hong Kong and coastal areas of Guangdong Province : Hygrobates aloisii sp . nov . Males and females , as well as deutonymphs and larvae are described .
