The main objectives of this study were ( i ) to provide an updated age - length key for D . trunculus from Atlantic waters , and ( ii ) to evaluate population dynamics , the timing of recruitment and production parameters of the wedge clam Donax trunculus in two moderately exposed sandy beaches ( the protected area of Doana National Park and the tourist destination of Isla Canela ) along the littoral of Huelva ( SW Spain ) .
