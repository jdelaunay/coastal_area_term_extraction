This article takes China ' s coastal areas as the research object , and on the basis of analyzing the development status of its health and wellness tourism industry , it proposes that China ' s coastal areas should start from tapping its own resource endowment characteristics to carry out innovative design of products , and build competitive advantages through industry ' s innovative operations to make full use of China ' s unique coastline resources and industrial chain to create an international route for coastal health and wellness tourism and promote the innovative development of the entire industrial structure .
